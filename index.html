--- layout: default title: CIBOLib ---
Computational bilevel optimization for models including binary or general integer variables have been a highly active field of research over the last years and decades. To propel the development of new algorithms, it is very important for the community to have access to a large and well-curated set of test instances that can be used to test new algorithms and to compare different methods. There are very many success stories of such instance collections such as MIPLIB or QPLIB. The aim of CIBOLib and this website is to provide such a test instance collection for (mixed-)integer bilevel optimization.
